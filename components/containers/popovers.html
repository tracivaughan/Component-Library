Usage notes

  • Popovers are triggered by buttons. Press Escape to close, or click outside.
  • Backdrop control: Add data-backdrop="false" to disable the overlay (more typical for popovers), or data-backdrop="true" to enable it (better for modal-like behavior). Default is enabled.
  • Size control: Add size classes: ptn-popover-small, ptn-popover-medium (default), ptn-popover-large, ptn-popover-wide, ptn-popover-full, or ptn-popover-auto.
